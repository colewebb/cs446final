Ellensburg KOA Journey

Check into the cozy Deluxe Cabins, one of the Tipi's or settle into a prime spot right on the river and relax. You're well-situated to search for the Ellensburg blue agate, enjoy a concert at the Gorge Amphitheatre, hike the John Wayne Pioneer Trail, or visit Roslyn, where Northern Exposure was filmed. Central Washington University's campus is also located in town.-Ellensburg KOA Journey
