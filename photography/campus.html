CWU Campus

While its appeal as a place for photography may not be obvious, especially to those who spend vast amounts of time there already, the CWU Ellensburg Campus merits a mention because of the vast variety of opportunities presented. From the greenery of the Arboretum to the techno-chic of the Samuelson building, there is truly a place for everyone.
