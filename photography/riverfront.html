Irene Rinehart Riverfront Park

The picturesque views of a beautiful lake and the Yakima river make for a perfect backdrop to an outdoor photo session. The park, while it can be busy during the day, is less busy in mornings and evenings and boasts a number of locations for taking photographs in relative privacy.

Go!
