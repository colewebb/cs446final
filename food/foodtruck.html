CWU Food Trucks

The CWU Food Trucks are a wonderful option for those who have a meal plan but are tired of the fare at the SURC. Almost everything in the food trucks is cooked from fresh or raw, ensuring fresh food every time. The fare is also often foreign, making for an eye-opening culianary experience.

Go!
