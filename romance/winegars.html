Winegar's Ice Cream

Winegar's Ice Cream, home to some of the best ice cream in Ellensburg, draws couples in with its warm, cozy atmosphere and its promise of sweet confections. It is the perfect place to bring a date for a casual romantic evening of getting to know each other. Their delicious ice cream and vast selection of drinks is bound to have something for everyone to fall in love with.

Go!
