CWU Campus

CWU's Ellensburg campus provides a number of opportunities for romantic moments. There are many places for you and your loved one to have romantic conversations, go on long (or short walks) and spend time together generally. From the quirky atmosphere at the 1891 Bistro to the restaurant atmosphere of the North Campus Cafe, there's a little something for everyone.

Go!
